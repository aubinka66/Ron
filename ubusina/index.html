about us

White Horse Forestry, Inc. is owned and operated by AnnaMarie and Courtney White in the heart of the Bitterroot Valley, Corvallis, Montana.

Our goal is to enhance the Forest, on both public and private lands, in a manner that creates very little negative impact to the environment.
